# Final Report Template – Agriculture Monitoring Project

## 1. Analytical Statement
Farmers who monitor their farms regularly respond better to challenges.

## 2. Data and Variables
- NASA POWER: monthly climate stress indicators.
- FAOSTAT: annual crop yield.
- Primary monitoring dataset: farm monitoring frequency, response time, yield loss.

## 3. Descriptive Analytics
- Compare distributions of response time and yield loss by monitoring group.
- Summarize climate trend and yield trend.

## 4. Inferential Analytics
- Two-proportion test: effective response rate (regular vs irregular).
- Welch-style mean difference test for yield loss.

## 5. Predictive Analytics
- Multiple regression: yield_loss ~ monitoring_days + climate_risk.
- Time-series forecast: next-year yield using Holt linear smoothing.

## 6. Bayesian Justification
- Posterior Beta distribution for P(effective response | regular monitoring).
- Present 95% credible interval.

## 7. Conclusion
Use statistical evidence from LO1–LO6 to accept/reject the statement with limitations and future data recommendations.
